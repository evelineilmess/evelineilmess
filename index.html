(02) About

First and foremost a creative problem-solver. The "Swiss Army knife" in a team who bridges marketing and product, thinks strategically and brings strong creative input wherever it's needed.

Senior Graphic Designer and junior/mid-level Product Designer with over a decade of experience, now based in London. With the right team around me I can lead and navigate almost any creative project - static or motion, digital or offline - connecting the dots and driving the work forward with purpose and brand consistency.  I've collaborated with advertising agencies, creative studios and B2B/B2C partners across many industries; the industry itself is secondary, curiosity drives the work. I get deep into a brand fast, then translate ideas into visuals that resonate with the right audience.
