Imagined Futures

An immersive VR experience about hope, part of the UK's largest immersive storytelling project. I worked as UI / graphic designer on The Museum of Imagined Futures, shown in 15 UK libraries in summer 2022.

(01)Overview

The Museum of Imagined Futures is an immersive VR experience, and I played a part as UI / graphic designer.

It's a museum about hope: accompanied by Lumi, an AI museum guide, visitors explore how humans used to imagine our present, before time-travelling to the future themselves.

It launched on 1 July 2022 as part of StoryTrails, the UK's largest immersive storytelling project, with AR trails, spatial maps and VR experiences.

(02)The project

A landmark immersive commission.

The project was spearheaded by StoryFutures Academy, part of Unboxed, with partners including the BBC, the British Film Institute (BFI), Nexus Studios and The Reading Agency.

The Museum of Imagined Futures VR experience could be seen in 15 UK libraries in summer 2022 as part of the StoryTrails project, commissioned by Unboxed.

(03)My role

UI and graphic design for VR.

As UI / graphic designer, I worked on the interface and visual layer that carries visitors through the experience, from meeting Lumi to time-travelling between imagined worlds, keeping it clear and approachable for first-time VR users.
